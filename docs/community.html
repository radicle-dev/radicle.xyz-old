We out here

The community exists in various places. Join us!

Discord

Most conversations in the community are happening here. The core team is very active and typically responds to questions in real time.

discord.gg/HRdnwAwGbG

Discourse

The hub for all longer-form discussions about the project. The team uses it regularly to share project-wide announcements, releases, feature updates, and more.

radicle.community

Twitter

We use our Twitter to share announcements & project-wide updates, as well as amplify Radicle-aligned messaging from partnerships and the greater ecosystem. We don't offer support here.

@radicle

GitHub

All Radicle development is open-source and public by default. The radicle-dev GitHub is consistently active and is the best place to see what the team is working on. This includes roadmaps and planning documents (i.e. the Cycle Plan issue).

github.com/radicle-dev

Matrix

Some of the core team uses this to communicate, and our Discord is mirrored here, information shared here is basically the same as Discord.

matrix.radicle.community

Telegram

Larger announcements are posted in our official Telegram channel. There are also other channels sprouting up which are quite active.

t.me/radicleworld

t.me/radicleThailand

Stay up to date

If you'd like to stay updated about product releases, project updates, and other announcements, hand us your e-mail and we'll keep you in the loop.
