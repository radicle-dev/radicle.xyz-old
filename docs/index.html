Why Radicle?

It’s not easy to switch to something new, but here’s why you should.

Peer-to-peer

No servers. No platforms. No in-betweens. Share your code without relying on a third party.

Local-first

All of your issues, revisions, and reviews exist on your computer. You own your data — nobody else.

Secure

No need to trust anyone to keep your work safe. Everything is signed and backed by public key cryptography.

No more walled gardens.

Radicle is an open-source stack for code collaboration. Anyone can build on it.

Registry

For hosting & collaboration, we built Radicle Link, a peer-to-peer replication protocol built on git.

Link

For decentralized identity & project organization, we built the Radicle Registry.
